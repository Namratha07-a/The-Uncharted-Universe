Plot Summary
Nathan Drake is a street-smart young bartender in New York who moonlights as a pickpocket. His life changes when Victor "Sully" Sullivan recruits him to help find a treasure assembled during Ferdinand Magellan's voyage — and in exchange, Sully will help Nate find his missing brother Sam. Their quest takes them from Barcelona to the Philippines, where two 16th-century galleons hang suspended from cliffs. Competing against the ruthless Santiago Moncada and mercenary Jo Braddock, Nate and Sully must navigate betrayal, spectacular action, and the mystery of Sam's fate. The film works as an origin story — showing how a young Nate becomes the adventure-hardened fortune hunter of the games.
